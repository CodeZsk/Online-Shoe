How do I clean my Skechers shoes?

Please follow below Do's & Don't to clean shoes

Do's

Remove excess dirt from the upper and outsole by using a soft brush or towel.

Remove the laces and insoles (wherever possible).

Create a soapy mixture by combining water at room temperature and a small amount of mild detergent.

Place the footwear in the mesh laundry bag to keep them from banging around in your washer. Close the bag and drop the bag into machine and Select low spin option.

Rinse washes the footwear properly.

Dont's

Don't put footwear in the machine dryer.

Don’t use artificial means of drying.
